Why I am a developer...

I am currently a student General Assembly SF learning full stack web development. I am captivated by collaborating and creating. Coding is my passion. Not only do I get to create amazing projects out nothing, I get to collaborate and share ideas to make things better. This love for creating and enhancing the world around me is part of my everyday life; whether it's cooking, making pottery or just talking about ideas with my peers it motivates me. I strive to become a better full-stack devloper everyday by setting goals, learning new technologies, and applying them to projects. My love for front end development comes from my eye for design and my passion to be creative. Back end is alaways a rewarding challenge; creating databases, using tools and fitting all of the little pieces together.
